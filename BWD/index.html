about us

This site is an exploration into web design and challenging the web design norms that have been set in the genesis of the internet. Gaining inspiration from sites such as brutalist websites (RIP) and web design museum, this site acts as a showcase of websites created by creatives who do not conform to these norms, who are experimental, who are expanding horizons and potentionals, and are bringing the fun back into the web experience.
